03

Pack Opening

Qualifying action → pack → reveal → collect. Pack size configurable by operator.

Example Pack (3 fragments)

🇯🇵 Japan, Ball fragment Common
🇭🇷 Croatia, Boots fragment Uncommon

Every pack is a micro-event. Any fragment could be Legendary. As items and countries complete, probability redistributes to remaining ones, so progress accelerates the more you collect.

04

Country Tiers

Countries are grouped into 4 rarity tiers. The operator picks which countries go where and sets all drop probabilities from the dashboard. 16 countries included in scope.

Legendary

Lowest drop rate. Status symbols.

e.g. 🇧🇷 Brazil, 🇦🇷 Argentina

Aspirational. Completing one is a story players tell.

Rare

Low drop rate. Multi-week grind.

e.g. 🇫🇷 France, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇪🇸 Spain, 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿 England

Moderate drop rate. Steady progress.

e.g. 🇭🇷 Croatia, 🇳🇱 Netherlands, 🇵🇹 Portugal

Common

High drop rate. The on-ramp.

e.g. 🇯🇵 Japan, 🇲🇽 Mexico, 🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia

Quick wins. Players complete these first and get hooked.

06

Amplifier: Bring Your Crew

Turn every player into a channel for new registrations.

How it works
1

Player invites friends via personal referral code

2

Private friend leaderboard activates at minimum threshold (e.g. 10 active players)

3

Compete on who's the best WC bettor. We track all World Cup bets

4

Top 3 earn bonus packs as prizes. Value from thin air

Why it works

Acquisition: every invite is a potential registration. "Join my World Cup crew" spreads organically. Players do the marketing.

Engagement: friends competing against each other drives daily activity harder than any notification or push. Pure social pressure.

Minimum threshold protects the operator. Leaderboard only activates with enough active players to prevent abuse and ensures genuine competitive value.
